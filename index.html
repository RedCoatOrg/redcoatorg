Activating the Redcoat Message Filter on iOS

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Scroll down and select Messages.
  3. Scroll down and select Unknown & Spam.
  4. Swipe to turn on Filter Unknown Senders.
  5. On the same screen, under SMS Filtering select Redcoat.
  6. Select Enable from the pop-up dialog.
  7. Done!
